Process

What's with the construction icons?

I believe design is about working hard. I don't come from a formal design background, so I have to dig a little deeper. There is, however, a lot of hard work that I can build upon. Whether it's the d.school, IDEO, GV, Fresh Tilled Soil or one of the many other design leaders, they all follow some variation of the below process born out of agile and lean methodologies. Am I going to reinvent the wheel? Nope.

Approach

WE'VE GOT JOBS TO BE DONE

I've been beg, borrowing, and stealing from every design thinking resource I can get my eyes on. The group that has helped framed my approach the most is the team over at Intercom. Their approach is based on a combination of Clayton Christensen's Jobs to Be Done Framework and Jesse James Garrett's The Elements of User Experience.
